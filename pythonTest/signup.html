Fill out the form: In the sign-up form, you'll be asked to provide the following information:

  • Full name: Enter your first name and last name.
  • Mobile number or email address: Choose either option to use as your login credentials.
  • Password: Create a secure password that is not easy to guess.
  • Date of birth: Select your birthdate from the drop-down menus.
  • Gender: Choose your gender from the options provided (Male, Female, Custom).

Click "Sign Up": After filling out the required information, click the "Sign Up" button at the bottom of the form.

Verification: Facebook may ask you to complete a security check to ensure you are a real person. This could involve entering a verification code sent to your email or mobile phone.

Profile setup: Once you've completed the verification, Facebook will prompt you to set up your profile by adding a profile picture and cover photo, and finding friends by connecting to your email contacts.

Explore Facebook: You can start using your Facebook account to connect with friends, join groups, like pages, and share posts.
